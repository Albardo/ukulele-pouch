FAQ

Can I import my own PDFs?

Yes. Import PDFs into your library, organize them into categories, and search fast.

Does it work offline?

Everything works offline, including the songbook, chords, tuner, and metronome.

How do I backup or move data?

Use the export/import tools to move your library to a new device.

Does the tuner support High-G and Low-G?

Yes. Switch the tuning, and the tuner can detect the string automatically—or you can pick it manually.

Can the app detect a chord from the fretboard?

Yes. Tap positions on the interactive fretboard and the app will try to detect the chord name. You also get multiple fingerings and playback.
